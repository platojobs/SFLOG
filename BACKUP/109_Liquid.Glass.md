# [Liquid Glass](https://github.com/platojobs/SFLOG/issues/109)

Liquid Glass — The biggest shift in Apple’s design language since the flat redesign of iOS 7 in iOS 26 This is more than a simple cosmetic adjustment. It’s a new rendering philosophy.

Liquid Glass surfaces in the real world refract, reflect, and respond according to content beneath them and they do it all in real-time. This fundamentally changes the way you think about layering, contrast, and depth in your UIs as an iOS developer.
